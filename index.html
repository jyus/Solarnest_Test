Accelerating the transition to sustainable, affordable and accessible living

Who We Are

Solarnest is a Bristol based start-up with the vision of creating a cleaner, more sustainable future through sustainable building solutions. With a focus on design and marketing in the energy and construction sector, Solarnest want to change the way people live, as well as our relationship with smart energy for generations to come. We work with our own network of construction suppliers to create rapid deployment building solutions, meeting a broad range of commercial and non-commercial needs in the most efficient way possible.

What We Do

With a fantastic team of designers and entrepreneurs, we have already successfully created our first prototype of the Nest-i, a structured hub-like building that offers a wide range of commercial and domestic uses. We are introducing an affordable, mortgage free design whilst creating a fresh and sustainable, easy-to-manage space for our clients. Our environmentally friendly alternative to buildings does not need planning permission and can also be used to add value to the assets of landowners and property developers.

the nest-i

The Nesti-i is a stylish, rapid deployable housing and commercial unit, built offsite and assembled on site. The stunning design offers easy-to-access ownership, giving the owner the opportunity to create a passive income from high rental potential as a conventional B&B, a fresh space to work or relax in, or even act as guest housing for any kind of commercial or none commercial purposes.

Overall, the Nest-i is a transformable hub for your needs. Solarnest can tailor the Nest-i design to meet whatever requirements you have. The Nest-i solution is highly energy efficient and uses solar panels as an optional energy production unit whilst having the capability to store energy already collected.

If you are a property or land owner of holiday homes, retirement homes, country parks, guest houses or are a home developer looking for new options to affordable and sustainable living, Solarnest could provide you with the right solution.

Why Solarnest?

Solarnest’s prime focus is to create cleaner energy solutions. With the housing sector responsible for up to 30% of global energy consumption, our research proves that it is more harmful to the environment than even the transport sector. The current methods for housing has become detrimental to the wellbeing of our eco-system. Thankfully, however, Solarnest have a far less harmful alternative — the sun. At Solarnest, we plan to use the sheer energy of the sun to power over 30% of our energy usage for our homes, and maybe even more. This energy created by the panels on the Nest-i can both be used to power each unit, could be sold back to the market or even be used as a combination of both. If you think this is for you, send us an email, let us know what you think, follow us on social media and if you believe you could help with our movement into sustainable housing, then join us.

We have the technology and resources to live in harmony with our surroundings in a way which can also be sustainable both financially and environmentally. Together, we can help provide housing solutions and create a better world at the same time.
